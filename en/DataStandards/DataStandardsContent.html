Data Standards

Data entered for a specific attribute may be required to follow a specific standard. There are many standards that data entry could be required to follow and the Semantic Engine provides well formatted versions of the most popular ones.

When identifying the standard, use the best and most official standard identifier, for example URNs, PURLs, DOIs etc.

Here is a table of standards with some information about them:
